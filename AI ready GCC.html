  • Build Metrics: Success rates, build time improvements

Project Management

  • Jira Integration: Story point velocity, cycle time
  • Sprint Analytics: Completion rates, scope changes
  • Defect Tracking: Bug introduction and resolution patterns

Privacy & Compliance Controls

Critical: All data collection must comply with GDPR, local privacy laws, and enterprise data governance policies.
  • Data Anonymization: Personal identifiers removed from all metrics
  • Consent Management: Opt-in/opt-out mechanisms for telemetry
  • Data Retention: 90-day retention for sensitive metrics, 2-year for aggregated data
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to different data tiers
  • Cross-Border Data: Local data residency requirements compliance

Hyderabad-First Rollout Strategy

Phased Implementation Approach

Phase 1: Baseline & Preparation

Weeks 1-4: Foundation Setting

  • Establish baseline productivity metrics across all teams
  • Complete security and compliance assessments
  • Deploy Gemini Code Assist to pilot group (20% of developers)
  • Configure data instrumentation and monitoring
  • Conduct initial training sessions for early adopters
Success Criteria: 100% baseline data collected, zero security incidents, 90% pilot participation
Phase 2: Controlled Pilot

Weeks 5-12: Experimentation & Learning

  • Scale to 50% of development teams
  • Implement feedback loops and rapid iteration cycles
  • Conduct weekly retrospectives and metric reviews
  • Develop team-specific best practices and templates
  • Address identified issues and optimize workflows
Key Focus: Learning and adaptation, identifying what works best for different team types
Phase 3: Full Scale Deployment

Weeks 13-24: Organization-Wide Rollout

  • Deploy to 100% of development teams
  • Implement advanced AI features and integrations
  • Launch center of excellence program
  • Establish cross-team knowledge sharing initiatives
  • Measure and report on business impact and ROI
Target Outcomes: 85% adoption, 30% velocity improvement, $2.4M annual ROI
