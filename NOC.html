Real-Life Story: Critical Infrastructure Recovery

A case study demonstrating our next-gen NOC capabilities

The Scenario: Power Grid Cyber Attack

Date: March 15, 2024, 2:47 AM EST
Event: Sophisticated APT group launched coordinated cyber attack targeting our power grid management systems
Impact: Potential disruption to 2.3 million customers across three states
Challenge: Simultaneous network infiltration, malware deployment, and data exfiltration attempts

T+0:00 - Detection

Anomaly Detected

Our AI-powered anomaly detection system identified suspicious network patterns in the SCADA network. Unusual data flows triggered automatic correlation analysis across 47 different security sensors.

T+0:15 - Analysis

Threat Intelligence Correlation

QRadar SIEM automatically correlated events with threat intelligence feeds, identifying known IOCs associated with APT29. Machine learning algorithms confirmed 94% probability of coordinated attack.

T+0:45 - Response

Automated Containment

Self-healing capabilities activated: automated network segmentation isolated affected systems, backup systems engaged, and critical infrastructure switched to manual override mode. Zero human intervention required.

T+1:30 - Escalation

Human Expertise Engaged

L3 security engineers notified and engaged. VP and CTO automatically briefed via executive dashboard. Federal agencies (CISA, FBI) contacted through pre-established protocols.

T+3:15 - Resolution

Threat Neutralized

Advanced threat hunting identified and removed all malware. Compromised credentials revoked, systems patched, and network integrity restored. Full forensic evidence collected and preserved.

T+4:00 - Recovery

Full Service Restoration

All systems returned to normal operation. Zero customer impact achieved. Comprehensive incident report generated automatically, including lessons learned and recommended security enhancements.
