üéØ Core Philosophy: Infrastructure as a Product

We operate infrastructure as a product portfolio, not a collection of projects. This approach delivers three critical outcomes: speed to market, predictable reliability, and transparent unit cost governance.

Every infrastructure platform is treated as a product with:

  • Reference Architecture: Standardized blueprints that eliminate design iteration and reduce risk
  • Service Tier Rules: Clear SLA commitments tied to business criticality (Tier-0 through Tier-4)
  • Service Level Objectives (SLOs): Measurable availability, performance, and recovery targets
  • Unit Economics: Transparent cost per VM, per TB, per GPU-hour‚Äîenabling informed workload placement
  • Governance Rhythm: Monthly architecture reviews, quarterly business alignment, continuous optimization

This product-led model is the foundation of our operating discipline. It ensures infrastructure investment is defensible, scalable, and aligned to business value.

üåê Follow-the-Sun Operating Model

The organization operates on a Follow-the-Sun (FTS) model to deliver continuous global operations and support:

  • 24x7 Coverage: Three regional hubs (Americas, EMEA, APAC) provide seamless round-the-clock operations
  • Structured Handoffs: Standardized shift handoff protocols with incident summaries, priority escalations, and knowledge transfer
  • Tiered Support Model: L1 (triage & resolution), L2 (technical troubleshooting), L3 (subject matter experts) aligned across all regions
  • Global Command Structure: Unified runbooks, playbooks, and escalation paths ensure consistency regardless of time zone
  • Benefits: Faster MTTR, higher availability SLAs, improved business continuity, and reduced incident resolution times

This model enables us to respond to critical incidents within minutes, not hours, regardless of when they occur.
