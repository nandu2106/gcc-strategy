Key Discovery

Meeting Summarization is the Key Anchor

Data audits indicate that meeting summaries and transcripts constitute the primary value anchor for 80% of Copilot active seats.

By decoupling meeting transcriptions from interactive model reasoning, we can significantly reduce seat costs.

The Solution: Teams Premium ($6/mo)

Proposed Future State

AI Stack Alignment

We transition from a fragmented licensing model to a structured tiered strategy, optimizing costs while expanding AI access to all employees.

  • Tier 1: Copilot (100 Seats) - Reserved for executives and dedicated power integration workloads.
  • Tier 2: Teams Premium (500 Seats) - Broad deployment for meeting notes, transcriptions, and summaries.
  • Tier 3: Claude Enterprise (875 Seats) - Organization-wide standard for reasoning, coding, and document analysis.

Governance & Spend Controls

Claude Enterprise Cost Controls

Unlike standard SaaS seat billing, we protect the organization against unmonitored consumption by imposing granular policy boundaries inside the Claude workspace.

  • $50/User Cap: Hard consumption limit per billing period.
  • Scope Limitations: Access is restricted to Claude Chat and Cowork.
  • Code Safeguards: Claude Code is disabled for non-engineering teams.
  • Real-time Audit logs: Weekly exports tracking heavy user spike trends.

Governance Rationale

Establishing spend controls on day one ensures we do not experience budget overruns while baseline consumption behavior is measured during Phase 1.

Approval Flows

Users hitting 80% of their individual limit are prompted to submit an automated request to the AI Center of Excellence for additional tier allocations.

AI Governance Operating Model

AI Center of Excellence (CoE)

Rather than ad-hoc rollouts, governance is managed through a single central group with representation across departments.

  • CTO Sponsor: Secures funding, reviews escalations, and reports to the board.
  • AI Steering Committee: Establishes usage guidelines, evaluates compliance, and authorizes expansion.
  • AI Center of Excellence: Manages daily monitoring, allocates budgets, designs prompt templates, and runs user training.
  • Champions network: Evangelize use cases within business units.
