AI Privacy Protection

  • Federated Learning: Decentralized model training
  • Synthetic Data: Privacy-safe training datasets

AI Bias Detection & Mitigation
Bias Assessment Framework:
  • Statistical Parity: Equal outcomes across groups
  • Equalized Odds: Equal true positive rates
  • Individual Fairness: Similar treatment for similar individuals
  • Counterfactual Fairness: Decisions unaffected by sensitive attributes
Mitigation Strategies:
  • Pre-processing: Bias-aware data sampling
  • In-processing: Fairness constraints during training
  • Post-processing: Output adjustment for fairness
  • Continuous Monitoring: Ongoing bias detection
