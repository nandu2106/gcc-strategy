Metro & Access Resilience

Surviving Fiber Cuts and Local Disruptions

Metro Failover Logic

  • Every OLT must be reachable via two fiber paths
  • Use ring protection switching for instant failover
  • Use GIS tagging to instantly map probable cut location
  • Auto-ticket & field dispatch (Deck 2 automation)

Typical Fiber Cut Impact

  • 70–300 meters of last-mile fiber damaged
  • Impacts 500–3000 subscribers depending on area
  • Recovery time: 2–4 hours unless arterial route damaged

Reduction Methods

  • Pre-lay reserve fiber
  • Armored fiber in critical zones
  • Partner with municipal bodies for dig-alerts
