💡 “AI as a Product Portfolio” — measurable business outcomes, governed like a tier‑0 platform.

🎯 Strategy Thesis

In an ultra-big enterprise, AI succeeds when it is operated with the same discipline as infrastructure: reference architectures, service tiers, measurable SLAs/SLOs, and transparent unit economics.

Reality check at hyper-scale: Uber reports 5,000+ models in production and ~10M real-time predictions/second at peak, requiring factory-grade MLOps and safety guardrails. (Used here as a benchmark anchor.)

  • Value: tie each model to revenue, cost, risk, or productivity impact
  • Factory: standard build-test-deploy-monitor loop with automation
  • Governance: risk-tiered controls, audit-ready documentation, safe rollouts
  • Operating model: clear RACI, platforms + embedded squads
